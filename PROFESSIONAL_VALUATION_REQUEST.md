## Red Flags to Avoid

### Questionable Valuation Firms

âŒ **Avoid firms that:**
- Promise specific valuations before seeing your business
- Offer "too good to be true" high valuations
- Don't require detailed financial information
- Have no tech/SaaS experience
- Can't provide references or past valuations
- Use only one valuation method
- Don't explain their methodology

âœ… **Look for firms that:**
- Ask detailed questions about your business
- Provide range rather than specific number upfront
- Use multiple valuation methods
- Have technology industry experience
- Provide references and sample reports
- Are certified (ASA, NACVA, ABV credentials)
- Offer clear fee structure
