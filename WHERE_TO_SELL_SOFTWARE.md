## Success Tips

### Do's ‚úÖ

1. **Price Realistically**
   - Use professional valuation
   - Don't overprice by >20%
   - Be flexible on terms

2. **Be Responsive**
   - Reply within 24 hours
   - Be available for calls
   - Provide requested info quickly

3. **Professional Presentation**
   - Use your documentation
   - Create demo video
   - Professional pitch deck
   - Clean, organized materials

4. **Multiple Channels**
   - List with broker + direct outreach
   - Create competition among buyers
   - Don't be exclusive too early

5. **Transparency**
   - Disclose all issues upfront
   - Accurate revenue claims
   - Honest about limitations

### Don'ts ‚ùå

1. **Don't Rush**
   - Taking 6 months is normal
   - Don't accept lowball offers
   - Wait for right buyer

2. **Don't Overshare**
   - Always get NDA first
   - Don't share source code too early
   - Verify buyer legitimacy

3. **Don't Go Alone**
   - Use broker or attorney
   - Don't negotiate without help
   - Don't sign without legal review

4. **Don't Lie**
   - Never inflate metrics
   - Don't hide problems
   - Honesty builds trust

5. **Don't Get Emotional**
   - Business transaction, not personal
   - Be patient with buyers
   - Professional throughout
